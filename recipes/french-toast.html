French Toast

Description

This is the best French toast recipe. It's different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 (3/4- to 1-inch thick) slices of bread

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place flour into a large mixing bowl; slowly whisk in milk until smooth. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat. Meanwhile, soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
